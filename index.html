My Top 3 Anime Characters

In Bleach

Kurosaki Ichigo

A Substitute Shinigami with unmatched determination and inner power. He protects his loved ones by pushing past his limits.

Ichigo

Aizen Sosuke

A cunning former captain who betrayed Soul Society. With god-like intellect and power, he seeks to reshape the world.

Urahara kisuke

A brilliant inventor and exiled captain known for his wit and secrets. He plays a key role from behind the scenes with deep knowledge of everything.
